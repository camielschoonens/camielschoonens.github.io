This is my list of default apps. The categorization comes from the [Hemispheric Views Podcast](https://listen.hemisphericviews.com/097) and should be equal to all others submitting their default apps. More App Defaults are listed [here](https://defaults.rknight.me).

This is the third year I’ve been doing a Default Apps list. The categories here are based on the app categories discussed in [Hemispheric Views 097 - Duel of the Defaults!](https://defaults.rknight.me) podcast. I placed a 🆕 icon in every category where I changed the app compared to last year. There is also a [/Defaults](https://camiel.schoonens.nl/defaults/) page available on this domain.
